Welcome to the HACKAPARTY!

An immersive drinking experience based on the social media drinking game! Get some drinks, join a house HackaParty, or jump on an online HackaParty!

Join our newsletter to know about the future plans!

Score

The aim of the game is to score as many points as possible to beat all the other players. In order to win the HackaParty you not only need to write efficient code, but you also have to optimize your drinking skills!

Code

Solve programming challenges that will give you points relative to it's difficulty! The value of programming challenges will increase overtime, as it will become harder to solve them as you get drunker!

Drink

Your drinking skills will be tested with a set of drinking challenges that you'll have to fullfill for points. Points received from drinking will decrease as the party progresses.

Party

After the HackaParty challenge is over, everyone, especially the winners, will be more than ready to party. And for the adventurous ones - the HackaParty will continue all night!
